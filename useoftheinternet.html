Email

Email or electronic mail is defined as the transmission of messages over a communications network such as the internet. Just like the post office anyone set up to send and receive such messages has a name and address. The name and address take the form of xyz@companyltd.com where xyz is the name and companyltd.com is the address. Communications can be text typed on a keyboard or files attached to an email which open using programs such as word or excel. One of the most popular web applications is google's email gmail

Gmail

Communication

Communication over the internet is in many ways, e.g. email above is one. Other means of communication include chats in various formats such as Internet Relay Chat is text based, where users contact each other through a server. It is mainly used for group communications in discussion forums but can also be used for one to one communication. Online whiteboards are used mainly for education where teachers and students can interact over the internet. Internet users can also communicate through forums where people with similar interests share ideas and opinions through posts on the forum. Microsoft has an application called Microsoft Whiteboard used for online learning.

Microsoft Whiteboard

Research

There are many online tools, both paid for and free, to help researchers from research management and sharing tools such as Notero and Refworks to reference resources and information collection tools. Examples of reference resources are Google Scholar or Springer. Both databases have extensive an up to date scholarly work done on a variety of topics. Examples of information collection tools include Google Forms and Survey Monkey.

Google Scholar Survey Monkey

Ecommerce

Ecommerce is the buying/selling and payment for, goods and services over the internet between consumers and businesses and any combination of both. It pulls together many technologies from online marketing, data interchange and collection, electronic funds transfer, inventory and supply chain management and logistical management. the growth of ecommerce was impressive before the Covid pandemic but it has been further accelerated by this. It has brought about the terminal decline of some traditional businesses and represents a big threat to the survival of others. Amazon and EBay were 2 of the original ecommerce websites.

Ebay Amazon

Video Conferencing

Video Conferencing is a technology that allows participants in different locations to hold face to face meetings without having to all go to a single location. It is an economical way of bringing teams together, of having a client meeting or a product demonstration. Video conferencing tools such as Zoom and Teams became a business staple during the Covid Pandemic when travel was restricted and businesses wanted to remain operational.

Zoom

Voice over Internet Protocol

Voice over Internet Protocol or VoIP - also known as IP Telephony - is a technology that delivers voice communications over the internet. VoIP converts your voice to digital signals and the message travels over the internet. Calls can be made from a computer, a VoIP phone or a regular phone with an adapter. The digital signal is converted to voice before it reaches its destination. One of the best known providers of VOIP is Skype

Skype

File Sharing

One of the biggest advantages for businesses of the internet has been the ability to share relevant files with all parties who are involved in a project. Sharing can be public or private and how much access each individual has can be set - some may be allowed read only access while others may have full access including the ability to edit files. The three ways of sharing files are peer to peer as in the case of Napster which was shut down due to the sharing of copyrighted material, file hosting services such as dropbox whose servers are used exclusively to host clients' files. These can then be accessed and downloaded by users. The third way is through file sync and sharing - owncloud gives the user full control over their files and data.
